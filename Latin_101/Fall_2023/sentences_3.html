Sentences 3

Translate these sentences into English and be ready to explain your work.

1
Rōmae fuit.
  • Rōmae: PLN, locative case, Rōma, Rōmae f. Loc(X) = "at/in/on X"
  • fuit: perfect tense
2
fuit Neāpolī.
  • fuit: perfect tense
  • Neāpolī: PLN, Neāpolis, Neāpolī, f. ABL(X) without a preposition = "at/in/on X"
3
in Ītaliā fuērunt.
  • in: preposition + ABL(X) = "at/in/on X"
  • Ītalia, Ītaliā, f.
  • fuērunt: perfect tense
4
in prōvinciā mānsit.
  • in: preposition + ABL(X) = "at/in/on X"
  • mānsit: perfect tense
5
magnās cōpiās mīsit.
  • magnās: adjective
  • mīsit: perfect tense, principal parts = mittō, mittere, mīsī, _____
6
Caesar sē ad suōs recēpit.
  • sē: masculine singular accusative = "himself"
  • ad: preposition + ACC(X) = "to X"
  • suōs: adjective → substantive use (because it agrees with no noun in this sentence); = "his own"; because suos is MASC PLUR, translate "his own men"
  • recēpit: perfect tense
7
omnēs in castra perveniunt.
  • omnēs: adjective → substantive use (because no noun in the sentence agrees with it); = "all"; because omnēs is MASC PLUR, translate "all [the] men"
  • in: preposition + ACC(X) = "into X"
  • perveniunt: present tense
8
in nostrōs impetum fēcērunt.
  • in: preposition + ACC(X) = "against X";
  • nostrōs: adjective → substantive use (because it agrees with no noun in the sentence), = "our"; because it is MASC PLUR, translate as "our men"
  • fēcērunt: perfect tense
9
lēgātī profectī sunt cum nostrīs.
  • profectī sunt: perfect tense
  • cum: preposition + ABL(X) = "with X"
  • nostrīs: adjective → substantive use (because it agrees with no noun in the sentence), = "our"; because it is MASC PLUR, translate as "our men"
10
suās nāvīs ex portū ēdūcunt.
  • suās: reflexive adjective; it takes its meaning from the subject of the verb. Since the subject is 3rd person PLUR, suas = "their"
  • ex: preposition + ABL(X) = "out of X"
  • ēdūcunt: present tense
11

  • legiōnēs: ACC
  • ibi: adverb = "there/in that place"
  • cēterās: adjective
  • relīquit: perfect tense
12

  • omnīs: adjective
  • in: preposition + ACC(X) = "into X"
  • recipit: present tense
13
cum tribus legiōnibus est profectus.
  • cum: preposition + ABL(X) = "with X"
  • tribus: adjective, FEM PLUR ABL = "three"
  • est profectus: perfect tense, principal parts = proficīscor, proficīscī, profectus est
14

  • posterō: adjective
  • diē: ABL(X) without a preposition = "at/on/in X"
  • in: preposition + ACC(X) = "against X"
  • Nerviōs: PrN of a Gallic people, Nerviī, Nerviīs, m. plural = "the Nervii"
