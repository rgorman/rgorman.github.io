Sentences 4

Translate these sentences into English and be ready to explain your work.

1
eō diē mānsit Venāfrī.
  • eō: see is, ea, id
  • diē: ABL without a preposition indicate a point in time = "in/at/on"
  • mānsit: perfect tense
  • Venāfrī: pLN of a town in Italy, locative case, Venāfrum, Venāfrō, n.
2

  • in: prep. + ABL(X) = "at/in/on X"
  • iīs: see is, ea, id
  • fuērunt: perfect tense
3

4

5
