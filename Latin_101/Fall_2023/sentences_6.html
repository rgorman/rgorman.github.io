Sentences 6

Translate these sentences into English and be ready to explain your work.

1
Caesar impetum eius sustinuit.
  • eius: see is, ea, id
  • sustinuit: perfect tense; = "ward off"
2

  • Marsī: PrN of an Italian people, Marsī, Marsīs, m. plural = "the Marsians"
  • eam: see is, ea, id
  • occupant: present tense
3

4

  • mē: MASC/FEM SING ACC
  • Lūcullī: PrN, Lūcullus, Lūcullō, m.
  • dē: preposition + ABL(X) = "about/concerning X"
  • ipsā: = "the very"
  • mōvit: perfect tense; transitive not ergative; the verb frame is NOM(X) moves ACC(Y)
5

  • Crassus: PrN, Crassus, Crassō, m.
  • ad: prep. + ACC(X) = "to X"

  • contendit: present or perfect tense;
