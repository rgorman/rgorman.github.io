Sentences 7

Translate these sentences into English and be ready to explain your work.

1
ipsī ad mē dēdūcuntur.
  • ad: prep. + ACC(X) = "to X"
  • dēdūcuntur: present tense, passive voice
2
in Ītaliam profectus est.
  • in: prep. + ACC(X) = "into X"
  • profectus est: = "depart/set out"; perfect tense, deponent voice
3

  • haec: FEM SING NOM
  • dēlāta est: = "report"; perfect tense, passive voice
4

  • ex: prep. + ACC(X) = "out of/from X"
  • proficīscuntur: present tense, deponent voice
5

  • est ... dēlāta: = "report"; perfect tense, passive voice
  • ad: prep. + ACC(X) = "to X"
6
complūrēs hostium vulnerantur.
  • hostium: = "ememies"
  • vulnerantur: present tense, passive voice
7
dē quārtā vigiliā profectus est.
  • dē: preposition + ABL(X) = "from/after X"
  • vigiliā: = "watch"; Romans divided night time into four "watches" of equal length.
  • profectus est: ="set out/depart"; perfect tense, deponent voice
8

  • proficīscuntur: present tense; deponent voice
9

10

11

13

14

16

  • Eumenī: PrN of a Greek general, Eumenēs, Eumenis, m.
