Latin 102
Relatives, Conditionals, etc.

1
eae rēs grātiōrēs quam illae.
  • grātiōrēs: comparative, see adjectives 1
  • quam: coordinator, = "than"
  • illae: NOM FEM PLU
2
mundō autem certē nihil est melius.
  • mundō: ABL(X) without a prep. can = "than X"
  • melius: comparative, see adjectives 1
3
nihil est illī rē pūblicā cārius.
  • illī: DAT(X) can indicated a person affected by the situation, = "to/for X"
  • rē: ABL(X) without a prep. can = "than X"
  • cārius: comparative, see adjectives 1
4
sī tū exercitusque valētis, bene est.
  • sī: subordinating conjunction
  • -que: coordinating conjunction, Xque = "and X"
5
nam nihil istō potest esse iūcundius.
  • istō: ABL(X) without a prep. can = "than X"
  • iūcundius: comparative, see adjectives 1
6
semper aequē mihi amīcus fuit ac sibi.
  • ac: perhaps a coordinator; it introduces an equivalent comparison, = "as"
7
sē, simul ac potuit, ad Antipatrum recēpit.
  • simul ac: a complex conjunction (i.e., it consists of more than a single word), = "as soon as"
  • ad: prep. + ACC(X) = "to X"
  • Antipatrum: MAS SING ACC, PrN of a Greek man, Antipater, Antipatrī, m.
  • ē... recēpit: Idiom ≈ "retreat"
8
nihil aliud fēcerant, nisi rem dētulērunt.
  • aliud: an irregular adjective; for the pattern see here.
  • nisi: subordinating conjunction, = "if ... not/unless/except"
9
sī enim es Rōmae, iam mē adsequī nōn potes.
  • sī: subordinating conjunction introducing a conditional clause
  • Rōmae: FEM SING LOC, PrN of a Roman city, Rōma, Rōmae, f.
10
vōs nihil aliud nisi dē praesidiō rettulistis.
  • aliud: an irregular adjective; for the pattern see here.
  • nisi: can also sometimes be treated as a coordinator, = "except"
  • dē: prep. + ABL(X) = "about X"
11
ego minus saepe dō ad vōs litterās, quam possum.
  • minus: adverb = "less"
  • ad: prep. + ACC(X) = "to X"
  • quam introduces a comparison (non-equivalent), = "than"
12
nam Balbum posteā, quam tū es profectus, nōn vīdī.
  • Balbum: MAS SING ACC, PrN of a Roman man, Balbus, Balbī, m.
  • quam: introduces a comparison clause (non-equivalent); posteā quam = "later than/after"
  • es profectus: → perfect deponent of profīciscor: = "set out"
13
hōc mihi grātius in istā prōvinciā facere nihil potes.
  • hōc: ABL(X) without a prep. can = "than X"
  • grātius: comparative adjective, see adjectives 1
14
sed ego Thūcȳdidī crēdō, quod eius cīvitātis fuit.
  • Thūcȳdidī: MAS SING DAT, PrN name of a Greek historian and leader, Thūcȳdidēs, Thūcȳdidis, m.
  • crēdō: verb frame: SBJ trusts DAT(X)
  • quod: subordinating conjunction, = "because"
  • eius cīvitātis: functions as predicative; translate after verb
15
Capuae Nōnīs Februāriīs esse volēbam, quia cōnsulēs iusserant.
  • Capuae: FEM SING LOC, PrN of an Italian town, Capua, Capuae, f.
  • Nōnīs: FEM PLU ABL, PrN of a Roman calendar date; ABL(X) without prep. can = "at/in/on X"; Nōnae, Nōnārum, f. plural.
  • Februāriīs: FEM PLU ABL, PrN of a Roman month; Februārius, -a, -um.
  • quia: subordinating conjunction, = "because"
16
nōn tam multa in istīs rēbus intellegō, quam multa vīdī.
  • in: prep. + ABL(X) = "at/in/on X"
  • quam: relative adverb, = "how"; tam ... quam = "so ... as"
17
veniō ad recentiōrēs litterās, quamquam dē Serviō iam rescrīpsī.
  • ad: prep. + ACC(X) = "to X"
  • recentiōrēs: comparative, see adjectives 1
  • quamquam: subordinating conjunction, = "although"
  • dē: prep. + ABL(X) = "about/concerning X"
  • Serviō: MAS SING ABL, PrN of a Roman man, Servius, Servī, m.
18
tū, sī istīus modī sermōnēs ad tē dēlātī dē mē sunt, nōn dēbuistī crēdere.
  • sī: subordinating conjunction, = "if"
  • ad: prep. + ACC(X) = "to X"
  • dēlātī ... sunt: = "were reported"
  • dē: prep. + ABL(X) = "about/concerning X"
19
apertius autem haec agō ac saepius, quod Quīntus, meus frāter, lēgātus est Caesaris.
  • apertius: comparative adverb
  • ac: ="and"
  • saepius: comparative adverb
  • quod: subordinating conjunction, = "because"
  • Quīntus: MAS SING NOM, PrN of a Roman man, Quīntus, Quīntī, m.
  • Caesaris: MAS SING GEN, PrN of a Roman man, Caesar, Caesaris, m.
20
minus multa ad tē scrīpsī, quod exspectābam tuās litterās ad eās, quās prīdiē dederam.
  • minus: comparative adverb, = "less"
  • ad: prep. + ACC(X) = "to X"
  • quod: subordinating conjuction = "because"
  • ad: prep. + ACC(X) = "in reply to X"
  • quās: see relative adjective patterns
